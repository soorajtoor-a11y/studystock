HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — BEHAVIORAL HEALTH (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. The test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references below are OFFICIAL (verbatim from the HOSA guideline). Item counts = the official percentages applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official content area and to the listed references.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes (essay tiebreaker).
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Psychology, behavioral-health disorders and treatment, substance abuse, and wellness.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Cacioppo & Freberg. Discovering Psychology. Cengage, latest ed.
- SAMHSA website (samhsa.gov)
- National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) website

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. The Mind (biology, sensation, perception, consciousness, motivation, emotion, learning, memory) (13 items — 25%)
1. Explain the biological bases of behavior and the nervous system.
2. Describe theories of learning, memory, motivation, and emotion.

B. Psychology & Behavioral-Health Disorders, Treatments & Therapies (13 items — 25%)
1. Classify major mental-health disorders (DSM categories).
2. Describe common treatments and therapies (CBT, medication, services).

C. Prevention, Identification, Recovery & Management of Substance Abuse & Mental Illness (13 items — 25%)
1. Explain risk factors, screening, and stages of recovery.
2. Describe substance-use disorders and management approaches.

D. Wellness, Stress & Coping (7 items — 15%)
1. Explain stress physiology and healthy coping strategies.
2. Describe components of mental and emotional wellness.

E. Workforce/Careers & Laws, Regulations & Guidelines (2 items — 5%)
1. Identify behavioral-health careers and relevant laws (e.g., confidentiality).

F. History & Health-Systems Integration (2 items — 5%)
1. Describe the history of behavioral health and integration into care systems.
