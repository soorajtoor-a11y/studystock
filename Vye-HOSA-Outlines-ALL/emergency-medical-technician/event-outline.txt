HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — EMERGENCY MEDICAL TECHNICIAN (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: Skill event — Round 1 written test + Round 2 skill.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Prehospital emergency assessment and care.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Pollak (ed.). Emergency Care and Transportation of the Sick and Injured. Jones & Bartlett, latest ed.
- AHA BLS Provider Manual.
- Limmer. Emergency Care. Brady, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Patient Assessment (10 items — 20%)
1. Perform scene size-up and primary/secondary assessment.

B. Basic Life Support (10 items — 20%)
1. Perform BLS/CPR and AED per current guidelines.

C. Trauma (8 items — 16%)
1. Assess and manage traumatic injuries and bleeding.

D. Medical Emergencies (7 items — 14%)
1. Recognize and manage common medical emergencies.

E. Pediatrics & Childbirth (8 items — 16%)
1. Manage pediatric emergencies and emergency childbirth.

F. Environmental Emergencies (5 items — 10%)
1. Manage heat, cold, and other environmental emergencies.

G. Special Situations (2 items — 4%)
1. Respond to special situations and hazards.
