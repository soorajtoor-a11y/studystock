HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — MEDICAL ASSISTING (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: Skill event — Round 1 written test + Round 2 skill.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Administrative and clinical medical-assisting competencies.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Blesi, Wise & Kelley-Arney. Medical Assisting: Administrative and Clinical Competencies. Cengage, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Professionalism (2 items — 4%)
1. Demonstrate professional behavior and appearance.

B. Communication Skills (3 items — 6%)
1. Communicate effectively with patients and staff.

C. Anatomy & Physiology and Medical Terminology (5 items — 10%)
1. Apply anatomy, physiology, and medical terminology.

D. Legal & Ethical Issues (5 items — 10%)
1. Explain legal and ethical responsibilities (HIPAA).

E. Office Procedures (5 items — 10%)
1. Perform administrative office procedures.

F. Health Insurance & Coding (5 items — 10%)
1. Explain insurance, billing, and coding basics.

G. Infection Control (5 items — 10%)
1. Apply infection-control practices.

H. Collecting & Processing Specimens (5 items — 10%)
1. Collect and process specimens correctly.

I. Diagnostic Testing (5 items — 10%)
1. Perform basic diagnostic tests.

J. Clinical Procedures & Equipment (5 items — 10%)
1. Perform clinical procedures and use equipment safely.

K. Physical Examinations (5 items — 10%)
1. Assist with physical examinations.
