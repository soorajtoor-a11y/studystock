HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — PHARMACY SCIENCE (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: Skill event — Round 1 written test + Round 2 skill.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Pharmacy practice, calculations, compounding, and disorders/treatments.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Johnston. The Pharmacy Technician: Foundations and Practices. Pearson, latest ed.
- Moini. The Pharmacy Technician: A Comprehensive Approach. Cengage, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Pharmacy Practice History & Practice Settings (2 items — 4%)
1. Describe pharmacy history and practice settings.

B. Pharmacy Law, Ethics, Safety & Infection Control (5 items — 10%)
1. Explain pharmacy law, ethics, and safety.

C. Pharmacy Terminology & Abbreviations (5 items — 10%)
1. Use pharmacy terminology and abbreviations.

D. Dosage Formulations & Administration Routes (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe dosage forms and routes of administration.

E. Sterile & Non-Sterile Compounding (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe sterile and non-sterile compounding and aseptic technique.

F. Concentrations & Dilutions (5 items — 10%)
1. Perform concentration and dilution calculations.

G. Disorders & Treatments (23 items — 46%)
1. Relate common disorders to drug classes and treatments.
