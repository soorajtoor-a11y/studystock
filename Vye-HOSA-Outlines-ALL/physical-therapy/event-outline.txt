HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — PHYSICAL THERAPY (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: Skill event — Round 1 written test + Round 2 skill.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Physical therapy profession and care across condition categories.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Pierson & Fairchild. Principles and Techniques of Patient Care. Elsevier, latest ed.
- Pagliarulo. Introduction to Physical Therapy. Elsevier, latest ed.
- Simmers et al. DHO: Health Science. Cengage, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Profession of PT (7 items — 14%)
1. Describe the PT profession, roles, and settings.

B. Legal & Ethical Considerations (5 items — 10%)
1. Explain legal and ethical responsibilities in PT.

C. PT for Musculoskeletal Conditions (8 items — 16%)
1. Describe PT interventions for musculoskeletal conditions.

D. PT for Neuromuscular Conditions (8 items — 16%)
1. Describe PT interventions for neuromuscular conditions.

E. PT for Cardiopulmonary Conditions (7 items — 14%)
1. Describe PT interventions for cardiopulmonary conditions.

F. PT for Integumentary Conditions (7 items — 14%)
1. Describe PT interventions for integumentary conditions.

G. PT for Pediatrics & Older Adults (8 items — 16%)
1. Describe PT considerations for pediatric and older-adult patients.
