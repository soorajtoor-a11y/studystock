DECA STUDY OUTLINE — FINANCE CLUSTER EXAM (2025–2026)
Source: DECA Exam Blueprints 2025 + MBA Research DECA Exam Specifications (ICDC/national blueprint) for instructional areas and item counts; performance indicators from the official DECA Career Cluster Performance Indicators (MBA Research).
Format: Objective Test — 100 multiple-choice questions.
Used for: Accounting Applications Series, Business Finance Series, Financial Services Team Decision Making, Financial Consulting, and other Finance cluster events.
Item counts per instructional area (ICDC level) shown in (parentheses). Objectives are authentic MBA Research performance indicators for each area (a representative subset — expand from the official PI list as needed).
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Focuses on financial planning, analysis, and services — dominated by financial analysis and financial-information management, with professional development and risk management close behind.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Business Law (7 items)
1. Comply with the spirit and intent of laws and regulations
2. Discuss the nature of law and sources of law in the United States
3. Describe the United States' judicial system
4. Describe methods used to protect intellectual property
5. Describe legal issues affecting businesses
6. Identify the basic torts relating to business enterprises
7. Describe the nature of legally binding contracts
8. Explain the nature of agency relationships
9. Explain types of business ownership
10. Discuss the nature of debtor-creditor relationships

B. Communications (3 items)
1. Explain the nature of effective verbal communications
2. Demonstrate active listening skills
3. Explain communication techniques that support and encourage a speaker
4. Interpret others' nonverbal cues
5. Ask relevant questions
6. Explain the nature of effective written communications
7. Write business letters
8. Write persuasive messages
9. Make oral presentations
10. Participate in group discussions

C. Customer Relations (4 items)
1. Explain the nature of positive customer relations
2. Demonstrate a customer-service mindset
3. Develop rapport with customers
4. Reinforce service orientation through communication
5. Respond to customer inquiries
6. Handle difficult customers
7. Handle customer/client complaints
8. Interpret business policies to customers/clients
9. Build and maintain relationships with customers
10. Discuss the nature of customer relationship management

D. Economics (4 items)
1. Describe the concepts of economics and economic activities
2. Explain the concept of economic resources
3. Distinguish between economic goods and services
4. Determine economic utilities created by business activities
5. Explain the principles of supply and demand
6. Describe the functions of prices in markets
7. Explain the types of economic systems
8. Explain the concept of private enterprise
9. Explain the concept of competition
10. Describe the nature of taxes

E. Emotional Intelligence (6 items)
1. Describe the nature of emotional intelligence
2. Explain the concept of self-esteem
3. Demonstrate honesty and integrity
4. Demonstrate responsible behavior
5. Exhibit a positive attitude
6. Demonstrate self-control
7. Explain the concept of leadership
8. Participate as a team member
9. Use conflict-resolution skills
10. Explain the nature of office politics

F. Financial Analysis (30 items)
1. Explain forms of financial exchange
2. Describe functions of money
3. Explain the time value of money
4. Explain the purposes and importance of credit
5. Explain the concept of accounting
6. Describe the nature of cash flow statements
7. Explain the nature of balance sheets
8. Describe the nature of income statements
9. Explain the role of finance in business
10. Describe the nature of budgets

G. Financial-Information Management (12 items)
1. Explain the nature and scope of the financial-information management function
2. Explain the role of ethics in financial-information management
3. Describe the use of technology in the financial-information management function
4. Describe the need to accurately report a business's financial position
5. Demonstrate financial analysis applications
6. Demonstrate budgeting applications
7. Demonstrate data-mining techniques
8. Explain the use of financial information to identify trends
9. Discuss types of accounting systems used to report a business's financial position

H. Information Management (5 items)
1. Assess information needs
2. Obtain needed information efficiently
3. Evaluate quality and source of information
4. Draw conclusions based on information analysis
5. Discuss the nature of information management
6. Identify ways that technology impacts business
7. Demonstrate basic spreadsheet applications
8. Demonstrate basic word-processing skills
9. Describe the nature of business records
10. Interpret statistical findings

I. Marketing (1 item)
1. Explain marketing and its importance in a global economy
2. Describe marketing functions and related activities
3. Explain factors that influence customer/client/business buying behavior
4. Discuss actions employees can take to achieve the company's desired results
5. Describe connections between company actions and results
6. Distinguish between retailing and marketing
7. Explain the importance of merchandising to retailers
8. Differentiate between service marketing and product marketing
9. Discuss the relationship between promotion and marketing

J. Operations (4 items)
1. Explain the nature of operations
2. Describe the use of technology in operations
3. Describe health and safety regulations in business
4. Maintain a safe work environment
5. Explain routine security precautions
6. Explain the nature of project management
7. Explain the nature and scope of purchasing
8. Maintain inventory of supplies
9. Identify quality-control measures
10. Explain the concept of supply chain

K. Professional Development (15 items)
1. Maintain appropriate personal appearance
2. Adhere to company protocols and policies
3. Follow rules of conduct
4. Make decisions
5. Demonstrate problem-solving skills
6. Use time-management skills
7. Utilize job-search strategies
8. Prepare a resume
9. Interview for a job
10. Explain the need for ongoing education as a worker

L. Risk Management (9 items)
1. Explain the role of ethics in risk management
2. Describe the use of technology in risk management
3. Discuss legal considerations affecting risk management
4. Discuss reasons to integrate risk management into business operations
5. Discuss the nature of enterprise risk management (ERM)
6. Describe international considerations affecting risk management
7. Identify departmental business risks
8. Assess business risks
9. Integrate risk management into business operations
