HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — ALLIED HEALTH STATISTICS (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: ATC (Academic Testing Center) online test.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Healthcare statistics: census, rates, and data analysis.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Basic Allied Health Statistics and Analysis, 5th ed. (ISBN 9781337796965). Cengage.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Importance of Health Statistics & Data Across the Continuum (10 items — 20%)
1. Explain the role of health statistics across care settings.

B. Census, Occupancy & Length-of-Stay Statistics (12 items — 24%)
1. Calculate census, occupancy, and length-of-stay statistics.

C. Hospital Obstetric & Neonatal Statistics (4 items — 8%)
1. Calculate obstetric and neonatal rates.

D. Clinical & Non-Clinical Statistics (4 items — 8%)
1. Differentiate clinical and non-clinical statistics.

E. Mortality & Autopsy Terms/Rates (4 items — 8%)
1. Calculate mortality and autopsy rates.

F. Community Health Statistics (4 items — 8%)
1. Interpret community health statistics.

G. Math, Statistics & Data Organization (8 items — 16%)
1. Apply math and organize statistical data.

H. Research Fundamentals (4 items — 8%)
1. Explain research fundamentals.
