Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Medical device safety, electronics, and healthcare technology management.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- AAMI BMET 101 (AAMI Training).
- MET 100 Series (College of Biomedical Equipment Training).

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Anatomy & Physiology (5 items — 10%)
1. Relate anatomy and physiology to medical-device use.

B. Medical Devices as Related to Public Safety (11 items — 22%)
1. Explain medical devices and their safe operation.

C. Safety in Healthcare (8 items — 16%)
1. Apply healthcare and electrical safety standards.

D. Fundamentals in Electricity & Electronics (7 items — 14%)
1. Apply electricity and electronics fundamentals.

E. Reasoning (12 items — 24%)
1. Apply troubleshooting and logical reasoning.

F. Healthcare IT (7 items — 14%)
1. Describe healthcare IT and device networking.
