HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — CLINICAL LABORATORY SCIENCE (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: Skill/technical event — Round 1 written test + Round 2 skill.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Clinical laboratory disciplines, safety, and analysis.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Estridge & Reynolds. Basic Clinical Laboratory Techniques. Cengage, latest ed.
- OneLabREACH modules (safety, microscopy).

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Clinical Lab Professions (4 items — 8%)
1. Describe clinical laboratory careers and roles.

B. Biological Safety (6 items — 12%)
1. Apply laboratory biosafety and standard precautions.

C. Laboratory Math (3 items — 6%)
1. Perform laboratory calculations and dilutions.

D. Hematology & Hemostasis (7 items — 14%)
1. Describe hematology testing and coagulation.

E. Immunology (8 items — 16%)
1. Explain immunology principles and serologic tests.

F. Urinalysis (4 items — 8%)
1. Perform and interpret urinalysis.

G. Clinical Chemistry (7 items — 14%)
1. Describe clinical chemistry analytes and testing.

H. Clinical Microbiology (7 items — 14%)
1. Describe microbiology identification methods.

I. Parasitology (4 items — 8%)
1. Identify common parasites and testing.
