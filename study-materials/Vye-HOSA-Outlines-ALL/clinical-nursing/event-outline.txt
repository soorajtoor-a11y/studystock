HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — CLINICAL NURSING (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: Skill event — Round 1 written test + Round 2 skill.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Nursing care across the lifespan and by body-system disorders.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Perry & Potter. Clinical Nursing Skills and Techniques. Elsevier/Mosby, latest ed.
- Rosdahl & Kowalski. Textbook of Basic Nursing. Wolters Kluwer, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Basic Care (10 items — 20%)
1. Perform fundamental nursing care and safety.

B. Clinical Care Through the Life Span (10 items — 20%)
1. Provide care across maternal/newborn, pediatric, adult, gerontology, and mental health.

C. Clinical Care for Systems Diseases & Disorders (30 items — 60%)
1. Explain structure/function, signs/symptoms, assessment, pharmacology, nutrition, and treatments by body system.
