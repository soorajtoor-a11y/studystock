HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — CREATIVE PROBLEM SOLVING (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: Team leadership event — Round 1 written test + Round 2 team problem-solving performance.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Critical thinking, logic, and structured problem solving.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- McShane & Von Glinow. Organizational Behavior. McGraw Hill, latest ed.
- Adair. Decision Making & Problem Solving. Kogan Page, latest ed.
- Sternad. Solve It! Econcise, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Critical & Effective Thinking (13 items — 26%)
1. Apply critical- and effective-thinking principles.

B. Problem-Solving Strategies & Skills (12 items — 24%)
1. Apply structured problem-solving strategies.

C. Examining Constraints / Developing Alternatives (10 items — 20%)
1. Analyze constraints and develop alternatives.

D. Components of Logic (10 items — 20%)
1. Apply the components of logic and sound reasoning.

E. Clarification of Problems & Generating Ideas (5 items — 10%)
1. Clarify problems and generate ideas.
