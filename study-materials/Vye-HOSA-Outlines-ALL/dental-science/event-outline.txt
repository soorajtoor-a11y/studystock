HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — DENTAL SCIENCE (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: Skill event — Round 1 written test + Round 2 skill.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Dental team, anatomy, pathology, chairside assisting, and radiology.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Boyd. Dental Instruments: A Pocket Guide. Saunders, latest ed.
- Robinson. Modern Dental Assisting. Elsevier, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Dental Team, Specialties & History (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe the dental team, specialties, and history.

B. Dental Anatomy & Physiology (5 items — 10%)
1. Identify oral and dental anatomy and physiology.

C. Dental Pathology (5 items — 10%)
1. Recognize common dental diseases and conditions.

D. Tooth Morphology (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe tooth morphology and numbering systems.

E. Infection Control / Occupational Health & Safety (5 items — 10%)
1. Apply dental infection control and OSHA safety.

F. Chairside Assisting / Instruments (incl. Restorative & Lab Materials) (15 items — 30%)
1. Identify instruments and chairside procedures.
2. Describe restorative and laboratory materials.

G. Radiology (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe dental radiography principles and safety.

H. Medical Emergencies in the Dental Office (5 items — 10%)
1. Respond to medical emergencies in the dental office.
