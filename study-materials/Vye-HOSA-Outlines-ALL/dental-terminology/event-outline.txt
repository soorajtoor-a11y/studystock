HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — DENTAL TERMINOLOGY (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = the official percentages applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official content area and the listed references.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes (20 tiebreaker questions).
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Terminology of comprehensive dental care, specialties, instruments, and procedures.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Kindle & Dofka. Dental Terminology. Cengage, latest ed.
- Nelson. Wheeler's Dental Anatomy, Physiology and Occlusion. Elsevier, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Comprehensive Dental Care & Dental Specialties (15 items — 30%)
1. Use terminology for dental specialties and comprehensive care.
2. Use terminology for dental procedures and treatments.

B. Dental Instruments (5 items — 10%)
1. Identify dental instruments and their uses.

C. Pharmacology & Pain Management (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe dental pharmacology and pain-management terminology.

D. Emergency Care (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe dental emergency terminology.

E. Tooth Restorations (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe restorative dentistry terms and materials.

F. Head & Neck Anatomy (5 items — 10%)
1. Identify head/neck and oral anatomy terminology.

G. Radiography (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe dental radiography terminology.

H. Dental Laboratory Materials & Procedures (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe dental laboratory materials and procedures.
