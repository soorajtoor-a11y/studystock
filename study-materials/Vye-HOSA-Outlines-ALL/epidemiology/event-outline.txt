HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — EPIDEMIOLOGY (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = the official percentages applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official content area and the listed references.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes (essay tiebreaker). A longer 75-question online round may be used as a qualifier.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Principles of epidemiology, disease surveillance, and public-health response.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- PH 101 Series: An Introduction to Epidemiology
- ActivEpi
- CDC: Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report (MMWR)
- ATSDR Environmental Medicine Education resources

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Introduction to Epidemiology (15 items — 30%)
1. Define epidemiology, incidence, prevalence, morbidity, and mortality.
2. Explain study designs and measures of association.
3. Explain the epidemiologic triangle and chain of infection.

B. World & Government Response to Infectious Diseases (10 items — 20%)
1. Describe the roles of WHO, CDC, and public-health agencies.
2. Explain outbreak investigation and response.

C. CDC: Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report (MMWR) (25 items — 50%)
1. Interpret data and reports from the CDC MMWR.
2. Apply surveillance data to public-health decisions.
