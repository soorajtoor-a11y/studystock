HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — HOME HEALTH AIDE (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: Skill event — Round 1 written test + Round 2 skill.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
In-home personal care, safety, and support across body systems and special populations.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Balduzzi. Homemaker Home Health Aide. Cengage, latest ed.
- Leahy, Fuzy & Grafe. Providing Home Care. Hartman, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Responsibilities & Legal/Ethical Issues (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe the home health aide role, legal limits, and ethics.

B. Effective Communication (3 items — 6%)
1. Communicate professionally with clients and the care team.

C. Infection Control & Safety (5 items — 10%)
1. Apply infection control and home safety practices.

D. Body Systems & Common Disorders (20 items — 40%)
1. Identify body systems and common disorders and their care.

E. Caring for Special Populations (12 items — 24%)
1. Provide care for elderly, pediatric, and special-needs clients.

F. Basic Healthcare Skills (5 items — 10%)
1. Perform basic healthcare skills (vital signs, mobility, ADLs).
