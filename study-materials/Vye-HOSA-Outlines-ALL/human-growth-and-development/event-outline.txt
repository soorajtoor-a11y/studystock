HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — HUMAN GROWTH & DEVELOPMENT (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. The test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references below are OFFICIAL (verbatim from the HOSA guideline). Item counts = the official percentages applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official content area and to the listed references.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes (essay tiebreaker).
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Human development across the lifespan and the influences that shape it.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Polan & Taylor. Journey Across the Lifespan. F.A. Davis, latest ed.
- Simmers et al. DHO: Health Science. Cengage, latest ed.
- Kato. Lifespan Development. Goodheart-Willcox, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Government, Cultural & Family Influences (10 items — 20%)
1. Explain how family, culture, and policy shape development.
2. Describe socioeconomic influences on health and development.

B. Theories of Development (8 items — 16%)
1. Summarize major developmental theories (Erikson, Piaget, Kohlberg, Maslow).
2. Compare stage-based vs. continuous views of development.

C. Prenatal & Infancy (6 items — 12%)
1. Describe prenatal stages and factors affecting fetal development.
2. Describe infant physical, cognitive, and social milestones.

D. Childhood & Adolescence (11 items — 22%)
1. Describe developmental milestones of childhood and adolescence.
2. Explain puberty and identity development.

E. Adulthood (8 items — 15%)
1. Describe physical and psychosocial changes in early and middle adulthood.

F. Geriatrics, End of Life & Grief (7 items — 15%)
1. Describe aging changes and end-of-life care.
2. Explain the stages of grief and loss.
