HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — MATH FOR HEALTH PROFESSIONALS (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: ATC (Academic Testing Center) online test.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Healthcare mathematics: conversions, dosages, and flow rates.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Math for Health Care Professionals, 2nd ed. (ISBN 9781305509788). Cengage.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Proper Solution Creation (10 items — 20%)
1. Prepare solutions using ratio and proportion.

B. Basic Mathematical Concepts & Conversions (11 items — 22%)
1. Apply fractions, decimals, percentages, and conversions.

C. Drug Dosage Determination (15 items — 30%)
1. Calculate drug dosages accurately.

D. Input & Output Measurement (2 items — 4%)
1. Calculate intake and output.

E. Roman Numeral Conversion (1 item — 2%)
1. Convert Roman numerals.

F. Drops per Minute & Flow Rate (5 items — 10%)
1. Calculate IV drops per minute and flow rates.

G. Metric-to-English Conversion (5 items — 10%)
1. Convert between metric and English systems.

H. Basic Medical Terminology (1 item — 2%)
1. Use basic medical terminology in calculations.
