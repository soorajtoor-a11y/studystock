HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — MEDICAL SPELLING (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. NOTE: HOSA publishes NO weighted percentage test plan for Medical Spelling — questions are drawn from the official reference dictionaries (spelling and definitions of medical terms). No item-count-per-area breakdown exists.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes, followed by a live spelldown; the written test score breaks spelldown ties.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Correct spelling and definitions of medical terms drawn from the official references.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Taber's Cyclopedic Medical Dictionary. F.A. Davis, latest ed.
- Taber's Audio Glossary. F.A. Davis, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES
(No official percentage weighting — study the reference dictionaries broadly.)

A. Spelling of Medical Terms
1. Spell medical terms correctly across all body systems and specialties.
2. Spell terms for diseases, procedures, instruments, and abbreviations.

B. Definitions of Medical Terms
1. Match medical terms to their correct definitions.
2. Recognize roots, prefixes, and suffixes that inform spelling and meaning.
