HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — NURSING ASSISTING (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: Skill event — Round 1 written test (below) + Round 2 skill.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Foundational nursing-assistant care, safety, and body systems.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Dugan. Nursing Assisting: A Foundation in Caregiving. Hartman, latest ed.
- Roe. The Nursing Assistant: Essentials of Holistic Care. Goodheart-Willcox, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Nursing Assistant Roles & Responsibilities (3 items — 6%)
1. Describe the role, scope, and legal/ethical duties of the nursing assistant.

B. Anatomy & Physiology (10 items — 20%)
1. Identify body systems and their normal structure and function.

C. Infection Control / Safety (7 items — 14%)
1. Apply standard precautions and maintain a safe environment.

D. Communication / Culture (5 items — 10%)
1. Communicate effectively and respect resident diversity and rights.

E. Providing & Reporting Care (15 items — 30%)
1. Provide activities of daily living care.
2. Observe, document, and report changes accurately.

F. Disabilities & Disorders (10 items — 20%)
1. Describe common disorders and appropriate supportive care.
