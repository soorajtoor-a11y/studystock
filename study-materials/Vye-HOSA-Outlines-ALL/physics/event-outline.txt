HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — PHYSICS (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA NGL/Cengage Academic Testing Center Guidelines 2025-2026. Test plan (content-area %) and reference are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes (1 tiebreaker). Type: ATC (Academic Testing Center) textbook test.

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Mechanics, waves, electricity, magnetism, optics, and thermodynamics.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Serway & Vuille. College Physics, AP Edition, 12th ed. (ISBN 9780357976425). Cengage/NGL. (Event listed as 'Physics College'.)

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Work, Energy & Particle Dynamics (5 items — 10%)
1. Apply velocity, acceleration, force, and energy concepts.

B. Waves, Sound & Thermodynamics Laws (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe wave and sound behavior and thermodynamic laws.

C. Fluid Mechanics & Buoyancy (2 items — 4%)
1. Explain fluid pressure and buoyancy.

D. Electricity (current, resistance, circuits, fields) (10 items — 20%)
1. Analyze current, resistance, circuits, and electric fields.

E. Light Properties (wave vs. particle) (6 items — 12%)
1. Explain the wave and particle models of light.

F. Optics, Images & Focal Points (2 items — 4%)
1. Describe image formation and focal points.

G. Thermodynamics Laws (5 items — 10%)
1. State and apply the laws of thermodynamics.

H. Magnetism & Inductance (5 items — 10%)
1. Explain magnetism and electromagnetic induction.

I. Basic Physics Mathematics (10 items — 20%)
1. Apply the mathematics used in physics problem-solving.
