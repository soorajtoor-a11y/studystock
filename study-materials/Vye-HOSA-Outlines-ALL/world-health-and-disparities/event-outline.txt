HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — WORLD HEALTH & DISPARITIES (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes. Type: Knowledge test (in-person written test + essay tiebreaker).
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Health, health disparities, and strategies to reduce them.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Barr. Health Disparities in the US. Johns Hopkins Univ. Press, latest ed.
- Our World in Data (website).
- National Institute on Minority Health and Health Disparities (NIMHD).
- World Health Organization — Health Equity.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. What Is Health (10 items — 20%)
1. Define health and determinants of health.

B. Health Disparities & Diversity (20 items — 40%)
1. Explain health disparities and their causes across populations.
2. Describe social determinants of health.

C. Disparities in Treatment & Medical Decisions (10 items — 20%)
1. Explain disparities in treatment and medical decision-making.

D. Reducing Health Disparities (10 items — 20%)
1. Describe strategies to reduce health disparities.
