DECA STUDY OUTLINE — HOSPITALITY & TOURISM CLUSTER EXAM (2024–2025)
Source: MBA Research DECA Exam Specifications 2024-2025 (national/ICDC blueprint).
Format: Objective Test — 100 multiple-choice questions (includes BA Core).
Used for: Hospitality Services, Travel & Tourism, Hotel & Lodging Management,
Quick Serve Restaurant Management, Restaurant & Food Service Management,
Hospitality & Tourism Professional Selling.
Item counts per instructional area (ICDC level) shown in (parentheses).
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
The Hospitality & Tourism Cluster exam covers the Business Administration Core
plus the hospitality functions: customer relations, operations, information
management, and selling within lodging, food service, travel, and tourism.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Business Law (2 items)
1. Explain regulations affecting the hospitality and tourism industry.
2. Describe liability and safety law in hospitality settings.

B. Communications (3 items)
1. Communicate effectively with guests and coworkers.
2. Handle guest inquiries and requests professionally.

C. Customer Relations (9 items)
1. Explain the nature of positive customer/guest relations.
2. Demonstrate a guest-service mindset.
3. Handle guest complaints and service-recovery situations.
4. Build guest loyalty and repeat business.
5. Explain the role of customer service in the guest experience.
6. Anticipate and exceed guest expectations.
7. Adapt service to diverse guest needs and cultures.

D. Economics (5 items)
1. Explain supply and demand in hospitality markets.
2. Explain the economic impact of tourism.
3. Describe factors that affect travel and tourism demand.

E. Emotional Intelligence (7 items)
1. Foster positive working relationships in a service team.
2. Demonstrate self-control and professionalism under pressure.
3. Manage conflict and demonstrate empathy toward guests.
4. Lead and motivate a hospitality team.

F. Financial Analysis (7 items)
1. Interpret financial information used in hospitality operations.
2. Explain the nature of budgets and cost control in food and lodging.
3. Calculate food/beverage cost percentages and occupancy metrics.
4. Explain revenue management in hospitality.

G. Human Resources Management (1 item)
1. Explain the role of human resources in staffing hospitality operations.

H. Information Management (15 items)
1. Use technology and information systems in hospitality.
2. Explain the nature of property management systems (PMS).
3. Explain the use of reservation and point-of-sale systems.
4. Manage guest data while protecting privacy.
5. Explain the role of online reviews and reputation management.
6. Use data to forecast demand and occupancy.
7. Explain the nature of hospitality record-keeping.

I. Knowledge Management (1 item)
1. Explain the nature of knowledge management in hospitality organizations.

J. Market Planning (2 items)
1. Explain target markets in hospitality and tourism.
2. Develop marketing objectives for a hospitality business.

K. Marketing (2 items)
1. Explain the marketing mix as applied to hospitality and tourism.
2. Explain the role of destination and experience marketing.

L. Operations (13 items)
1. Explain the nature of operations in lodging and food service.
2. Comply with health, safety, and sanitation regulations (e.g., ServSafe).
3. Manage front-of-house and back-of-house operations.
4. Explain inventory and purchasing in food service.
5. Describe the guest cycle (reservation, arrival, stay, departure).
6. Explain quality and consistency in service delivery.
7. Manage housekeeping and facilities operations.
8. Explain risk and security management in hospitality.

M. Pricing (1 item)
1. Explain pricing strategies for hospitality products/services.

N. Product/Service Management (9 items)
1. Explain the nature of hospitality products and services.
2. Describe the guest experience as a product.
3. Explain menu and service design.
4. Explain branding and positioning of hospitality businesses.
5. Explain quality management of hospitality services.

O. Professional Development (6 items)
1. Explain career opportunities in hospitality and tourism.
2. Utilize job-search and networking techniques.
3. Participate in career planning and ongoing education.

P. Promotion (3 items)
1. Explain the promotional mix in hospitality and tourism.
2. Explain the role of digital and social-media marketing in hospitality.

Q. Quality Management (1 item)
1. Explain the nature of quality management in service delivery.

R. Risk Management (2 items)
1. Explain risk management in hospitality operations.
2. Describe the role of insurance and liability management.

S. Selling (9 items)
1. Explain the nature and scope of selling in hospitality and tourism.
2. Determine guest needs and recommend products/services.
3. Describe the steps of the selling process.
4. Perform up-selling and suggestive selling.
5. Handle objections and close the sale.
6. Explain group and event sales in hospitality.

T. Strategic Management (2 items)
1. Explain the nature of strategic management in hospitality businesses.
2. Conduct a SWOT analysis for a hospitality operation.
