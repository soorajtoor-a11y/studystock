FBLA STUDY OUTLINE — ECONOMICS (2025–2026)
Source: Official FBLA Competitive Events Guidelines (updated Nov 2025)
Format: Objective Test — 100 multiple-choice questions, 50 minutes
Test-item counts per knowledge area shown in (parentheses).
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Key economic concepts and principles: supply and demand, market
structures, fiscal and monetary policy, and the global economy.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Fundamental Economic Concepts (15 test items)
1. Distinguish between economic goods and services.
2. Explain the concept of economic resources.
3. Describe the concepts of economics and economic activities.
4. Determine economic utilities created by business activities.
5. Explain the principles of supply and demand.
6. Describe the functions of prices in markets.

B. Nature of Business (15 test items)
1. Explain the role of business in society.
2. Describe the types of business activities.
3. Describe the types of business models.
4. Explain the organizational design of businesses.
5. Discuss the global environment in which businesses operate.
6. Describe factors that affect the business environment.
7. Explain the nature of business ethics.
8. Explain how organizations adapt to today's markets.

C. Economic Systems (15 test items)
1. Explain the types of economic systems.
2. Identify the impact of small business/entrepreneurship on market economies.
3. Explain the concept of private enterprise.
4. Identify factors affecting a business's profit.
5. Determine factors affecting business risk.
6. Explain the concept of competition.

D. Government's Impact on Business (5 test items)
1. Determine the relationship between government and business.
2. Describe the nature of taxes.

E. Cost/Profit Relationships (10 test items)
1. Explain the concept of productivity.
2. Analyze impact of specialization/division of labor on productivity.
3. Explain the concept of organized labor and business.
4. Explain the impact of the law of diminishing returns.

F. Economic Indicators (15 test items)
1. Discuss consumer spending as an economic indicator.
2. Describe the economic impact of inflation on business.
3. Explain the concept of Gross Domestic Product (GDP).
4. Discuss the impact of a nation's unemployment rates.
5. Explain the economic impact of interest-rate fluctuations.
6. Determine the impact of business cycles on business activities.

G. Global Trade (25 test items)
1. Explain the nature of global trade.
2. Discuss the impact of globalization on business.
3. Describe determinants of exchange rates and effects on the domestic economy.
4. Explain cultural considerations impacting global business relations.
5. Discuss the impact of cultural and social environments on global trade.
6. Describe the impact of electronic communication tools on global business.
7. Explain the impact of major trade alliances on business activities.
8. Describe the impact of the political environment on world trade.
9. Explain the impact of geography on world trade.
10. Describe the impact of a country's history on world trade.
11. Explain the impact of a country's economic development on world trade.
