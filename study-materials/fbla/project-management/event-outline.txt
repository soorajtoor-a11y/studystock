FBLA STUDY OUTLINE — PROJECT MANAGEMENT (2025–2026)
Source: Official FBLA Competitive Events Guidelines (updated Nov 2025)
Format: Objective Test — 100 multiple-choice questions, 50 minutes
Test-item counts per knowledge area shown in (parentheses).
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Key project management concepts: planning, scheduling, budgeting,
risk management, and team coordination.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Foundational Knowledge (10 test items)
1. Explain the nature of project management.
2. Describe the role of ethics in project management.
3. Discuss the role and responsibilities of project managers.

B. Initiating and Planning a Project (40 test items — the biggest section!)
1. Initiate a project.
2. Determine stakeholder expectations.
3. Organize and prioritize work.
4. Identify resources needed for a project.
5. Prepare a work breakdown structure (WBS).
6. Create a calendar/schedule.
7. Identify quality-control measures.
8. Develop a communications plan.
9. Develop a risk-management plan.
10. Develop a project plan.
11. Utilize project-management software.

C. Executing, Monitoring, and Controlling a Project (30 test items)
1. Execute and control projects.
2. Monitor projects and take corrective actions.
3. Coordinate work with team members.
4. Coordinate cross-functional teams toward project/company goals.
5. Motivate team members.
6. Manage the project team.
7. Manage the project schedule.
8. Organize and track project resources.
9. Apply project-management tools to monitor and communicate progress.

D. Closing a Project (20 test items)
1. Evaluate project results.
2. Recognize/reward others for their efforts and contributions.
3. Determine whether goals/objectives were met.
4. Document lessons learned.
5. Close the project.
