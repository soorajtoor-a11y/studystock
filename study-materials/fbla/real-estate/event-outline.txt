FBLA STUDY OUTLINE — REAL ESTATE (2025–2026)
Source: Official FBLA Competitive Events Guidelines (updated Nov 2025)
Format: Objective Test — 100 multiple-choice questions, 50 minutes
Test-item counts per knowledge area shown in (parentheses).
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
The real estate industry: property law, real estate finance, market
analysis, sales strategies, and ethical practices.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Real Estate Fundamentals (30 test items — the biggest section!)
1. Distinguish between real and personal property.
2. Distinguish among real estate agents, brokers, and realtors.
3. Discuss benefits of membership in organizations like the National Association of Realtors (NAR).
4. Describe real estate careers (property managers, leasing agents, mortgage bankers).
5. Describe residential and commercial niches (office space, single family, industrial, land).
6. Define basic terminology (appurtenances, liens, hereditaments).
7. Describe the nature of land use.
8. Describe the real estate licensing process.
9. Explain the real estate valuation process.
10. Describe the role and responsibilities of appraisers.
11. Explain the role and responsibilities of real estate entrepreneurs.

B. Laws and Regulations (25 test items)
1. Identify discriminatory housing practices.
2. Discuss Fair Housing laws and protected classes.
3. Discuss legally required property disclosures (asbestos, lead).
4. Identify federal regulations protecting home buyers.
5. Discuss state, county, and local regulations.
6. Discuss land use controls and zoning.
7. Discuss the nature of leasing and tenancy.
8. Discuss regulatory organizations in real estate.
9. Discuss fiduciary responsibilities.

C. Real Estate Transactions and Finance (20 test items)
1. Describe the real estate transaction process.
2. Explain approaches to real estate lending.
3. Discuss deeds and transfer of title.
4. Discuss financial institutions' involvement in real estate.
5. Discuss the secondary mortgage market.
6. Explain the nature of real estate investments.
7. Explain closings/settlements.

D. Ethics (5 test items)
1. Discuss truth in advertising.
2. Discuss ethical considerations in real estate.

E. Agency (20 test items)
1. Describe types of agents (seller's, buyer's, dual, sub).
2. Explain the nature of agency relationships.
3. Explain the role and responsibilities of sales agents.
4. Discuss agents' roles in transactions.
5. Differentiate agency in commercial vs. residential transactions.
6. Explain ways to advertise listings for sale.

OFFICIAL REFERENCE RESOURCES (from FBLA guidelines)
- MBA Research National Business Administration Standards (not required)
- Investopedia Real Estate; Kaplan real estate license requirements; Realty Publications "Real Estate Principles"
