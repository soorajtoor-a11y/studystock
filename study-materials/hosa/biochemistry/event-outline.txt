HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — BIOCHEMISTRY (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA NGL/Cengage Academic Testing Center Guidelines 2025-2026. Test plan (content-area %) and reference are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Biomolecules, membranes, and metabolism.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Bettelheim et al. Introduction to General, Organic and Biochemistry, 12th ed. (ISBN 9781337571357). Cengage/NGL.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Water Characteristics (5 items — 10%)
1. Explain the properties of water important to biochemistry.

B. Proteins, Amino Acids, Peptides & Enzymes (10 items — 20%)
1. Describe protein structure and enzyme function.

C. Carbohydrate Structures & Functions (10 items — 20%)
1. Describe carbohydrate structure and function.

D. Nucleic Acids (DNA/RNA) & Genetics (10 items — 20%)
1. Explain nucleic acid structure and the flow of genetic information.

E. Lipid Structure & Functions (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe lipid structure and function.

F. Biological Membranes & Cell Transport (3 items — 6%)
1. Explain membrane structure and transport.

G. Metabolism & ATP Production (7 items — 14%)
1. Explain metabolism and ATP production.
