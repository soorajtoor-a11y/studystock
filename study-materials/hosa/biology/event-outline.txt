HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — BIOLOGY (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA NGL/Cengage Academic Testing Center Guidelines 2025-2026. Test plan (content-area %) and reference are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes (1 tiebreaker). Type: ATC (Academic Testing Center) textbook test.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Cells, genetics, evolution, organism systems, and ecology.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Biology: The Unity and Diversity of Life, 15th AP ed. (ISBN 9781337408332). Cengage/NGL.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Study of Life & Levels of Organization (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe the characteristics of life and levels of biological organization.

B. Structures & Functions of Plant and Animal Cells (10 items — 20%)
1. Describe cell structures/organelles and their functions.
2. Explain cell transport, division, and energy processes.

C. Mechanisms of Genetics & Heredity (10 items — 20%)
1. Explain DNA, replication, and protein synthesis.
2. Apply Mendelian genetics and inheritance patterns.

D. Principles of Evolution (5 items — 10%)
1. Explain natural selection and the evidence for evolution.

E. Evolution & Biodiversity (5 items — 10%)
1. Describe classification and biodiversity.

F. Structures & Functions of Plant and Animal Systems (10 items — 20%)
1. Describe plant and animal organ systems and their functions.

G. Principles of Ecology (5 items — 10%)
1. Explain ecosystems, energy flow, and population dynamics.
