HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — HEALTH INFORMATICS (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026. Test format, content-area TEST PLAN (percentages), and references are OFFICIAL. Item counts = the official percentages applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official content area and the listed references.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes (essay tiebreaker).
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Management, analysis, governance, and security of healthcare information.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Zhou & Clack. Introduction to Healthcare Informatics. AHIMA Press, latest ed.
- White. A Practical Approach to Analyzing Healthcare Data. AHIMA Press, latest ed.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. History, Careers & Roles (4 items — 8%)
1. Describe health-informatics history, careers, and roles.

B. Information Systems in Healthcare (6 items — 12%)
1. Explain EHRs and healthcare information systems.

C. Data Analytics (14 items — 28%)
1. Analyze and interpret healthcare data.
2. Apply descriptive statistics and visualization to health data.

D. Data Integrity & Quality (4 items — 8%)
1. Explain data integrity, accuracy, and quality measures.

E. Data Governance (6 items — 12%)
1. Describe data governance, standards, and stewardship.

F. Health Information Privacy & Security (6 items — 12%)
1. Explain HIPAA, privacy, and data-security safeguards.

G. Ethics (2 items — 4%)
1. Describe ethical issues in health information.

H. Research Study & Design (6 items — 12%)
1. Explain research study designs and data-collection methods.

I. Emerging Technologies (2 items — 4%)
1. Describe emerging technologies (AI, telehealth) in healthcare.
