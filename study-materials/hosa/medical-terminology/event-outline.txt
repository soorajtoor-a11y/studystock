HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — MEDICAL TERMINOLOGY (2025–2026)
Source: HOSA Competitive Event Guidelines 2025-2026 (official test plan).
Format: Objective Test — 50 multiple-choice questions, 60 minutes (+ 20 tiebreakers).
Item counts per content area (from the official test-plan percentages) shown in
(parentheses).
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Knowledge of medical language: prefixes, suffixes, word roots, and combining
forms, plus terminology for anatomy, physiology, and pathology by body system.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Roots, Prefixes, Suffixes & Combining Forms (23 items)
1. Identify common prefixes and their meanings (e.g., hyper-, hypo-, brady-, tachy-, dys-).
2. Identify common suffixes for conditions (-itis, -osis, -pathy, -emia, -megaly).
3. Identify common suffixes for procedures (-ectomy, -otomy, -ostomy, -plasty, -scopy).
4. Identify word roots for body parts and organs.
5. Explain the use of combining vowels and combining forms.
6. Build and analyze (deconstruct) medical terms from their parts.
7. Form singular and plural versions of medical terms.
8. Interpret common medical abbreviations and symbols.

B. Overview of the Body (2 items)
1. Describe levels of organization (cell, tissue, organ, system).
2. Use directional terms, body planes, cavities, and quadrants/regions.

C. Skeletal System Terms (3 items)
1. Identify roots/terms for bones and joints.
2. Identify skeletal conditions and procedures (e.g., osteoporosis, arthroplasty).

D. Muscular System Terms (2 items)
1. Identify roots/terms for muscles and movement.
2. Identify muscular conditions and procedures.

E. Respiratory System Terms (3 items)
1. Identify roots/terms for respiratory structures.
2. Identify respiratory conditions and procedures (e.g., bronchitis, tracheostomy).

F. Digestive System Terms (3 items)
1. Identify roots/terms for digestive structures.
2. Identify digestive conditions and procedures (e.g., gastritis, colostomy).

G. Cardiovascular & Lymphatic Terms (3 items)
1. Identify roots/terms for the heart, vessels, blood, and lymph.
2. Identify cardiovascular/lymphatic conditions and procedures.

H. Nervous System & Special Senses Terms (3 items)
1. Identify roots/terms for the nervous system, eye, and ear.
2. Identify related conditions and procedures.

I. Endocrine System Terms (2 items)
1. Identify roots/terms for endocrine glands and hormones.
2. Identify endocrine conditions (e.g., hyperthyroidism, diabetes terms).

J. Reproductive System Terms (2 items)
1. Identify roots/terms for male and female reproductive structures.
2. Identify reproductive/obstetric conditions and procedures.

K. Integumentary System Terms (2 items)
1. Identify roots/terms for skin, hair, and nails.
2. Identify integumentary conditions and procedures.

L. Urinary System Terms (2 items)
1. Identify roots/terms for the urinary system.
2. Identify urinary conditions and procedures (e.g., nephritis, cystoscopy).
