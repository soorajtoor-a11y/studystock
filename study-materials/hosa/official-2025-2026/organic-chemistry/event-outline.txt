HOSA STUDY OUTLINE — ORGANIC CHEMISTRY (2025–2026)
Source: Official HOSA NGL/Cengage Academic Testing Center Guidelines 2025-2026. Test plan (content-area %) and reference are OFFICIAL. Item counts = official % applied to the 50-question test. Objectives are representative, aligned to each official area.
Format: 50 multiple-choice questions / 60 minutes (1 tiebreaker). Type: ATC (Academic Testing Center) textbook test.
============================================================

EVENT DESCRIPTION
Structure, naming, and reactions of organic compounds.

OFFICIAL REFERENCES
- Bettelheim et al. Introduction to General, Organic and Biochemistry, 12th ed. (ISBN 9781337571357). Cengage/NGL.

KNOWLEDGE AREAS AND OBJECTIVES

A. Electronegativity, Structure & Bonding (8 items — 16%)
1. Explain electronegativity, structure, and bonding in organic molecules.

B. Chemical Bonds, Shapes & Compound Naming (8 items — 16%)
1. Describe bond types, molecular shapes, and naming.

C. Stereochemistry (2 items — 4%)
1. Explain isomers and stereochemistry.

D. Substitution & Elimination Reactions (3 items — 6%)
1. Describe substitution and elimination mechanisms.

E. Aromatic Compounds & IUPAC Names (6 items — 12%)
1. Name aromatic compounds using IUPAC rules.

F. Alkanes through Functional Groups (13 items — 26%)
1. Identify functional groups and their reactions.

G. Carboxylic Acids & Derivatives (4 items — 8%)
1. Describe carboxylic acids and their derivatives.

H. Amine Naming (4 items — 8%)
1. Name amines and describe their properties.

I. Spectroscopy (2 items — 4%)
1. Interpret basic spectroscopic data.
